#CollabGamers - Information

The #CollabGamers is proyect made by Alt 4 Javier, It is about making music for Youtubers and / or Streamers which are not paid, the project will close in September 2021.

After the fall of the tuda album, Alt 4 Javier gathers the possibility of making songs for youtubers and / or streamers which due to copyright problems make it difficult to find songs, Alt 4 Javier gets used to almost all possible genres for get what they need and admire.

FAQ

Which songs can I use?

You can use any song that is shared on my Youtube Channel

On which platform can I use your music?

You can use our music on YouTube and Twitch - More platforms are coming soon.

Can I sing/rap over your music?

No, any alterations of the original songs that we release are not allowed. You cannot add lyrics, sample, or remix the music we release.

Can I monetize my video if I use your music?

Yes, we allow and encourage content creators to use my music. As long as you credit properly, you will be able to monetize your video.
